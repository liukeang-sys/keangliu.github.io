ICBSC is an international business simulation hosted by Cal State Long Beach, where university teams compete by running a simulated company across multiple rounds. Teams make real strategic decisions — pricing, production, marketing spend, R&D investment — and are evaluated on business performance. The 2026 competition runs January through April, culminating in a 3-day on-site intensive in Anaheim.
